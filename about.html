Our Story

Established in 2013, our journey began in a modest cubical office in Sukrawarpet with a dedicated team of three individuals. Over the years, we've grown exponentially and now proudly stand as a prominent player in the industry, boasting an established showroom in RS Puram and a dynamic workforce of 15 members. What started as a venture focused on Acrylic Solid Surface, commonly known as Corian Tops, has evolved into a diverse product range that spans over 15 offerings.

At the helm of this remarkable transformation is Ashish Nahata, a visionary leader who not only oversees the sales and marketing aspects but also effectively manages the entire company, steering it towards new heights of success.

Our commitment is rooted in delivering unparalleled quality in premium products at affordable prices, all while maintaining a personalized touch and embracing uniqueness. We strive to set industry benchmarks by consistently providing products that exceed customer expectations.
